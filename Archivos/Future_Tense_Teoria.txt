
		FUTURE TENSE / TIEMPO FUTURO

Hay dos formas para expresar el futuro: 'will' y 'going to'.

Usamos el futuro simple para acciones que van a suceder en un tiempo cercano.

		I 'will call' you tonight.
		I 'am going to call' you tonight.

		(Yo te llamare esta noche.)

		She 'will arrive' late.
		She 'is going to arrive' late.

		(Ella llegara tarde.)

		They 'will be' happy to see you.
		They 'are going to be' happy to see you.

		(Ellos estaran felices de verte.)
